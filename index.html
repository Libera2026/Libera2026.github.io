About the Workshop

Where Theory Meets Application

This workshop aims to bring together leading researchers from Europe and the Americas working on stochastic processes and their dynamics, stochastic control and optimization with applications across mathematics, physics, statistics, and machine learning. We would be honored if you could join us to present your recent work and share your insights with the community. This event is partially supported by the Marie-Curie network LiBERA.

Scientific Scope

Stochastic differential equations, mean-field games, optimal transport, reinforcement learning, stochastic control, and applications in finance, biology, and engineering.

The LiBERA Network

LiBERA is a European research network advancing the mathematical foundations of learning, control, and optimization in stochastic environments. This midterm meeting tracks progress and fosters new collaborations.

Who Should Attend

Mathematicians, engineers, and computational scientists working on stochastic systems — from PhD students to senior researchers — are warmly invited to participate.

Venue — Bogotá

The workshop will be held at a leading Colombian university in Bogotá, a vibrant academic hub at 2,600 m altitude in the heart of South America. Venue details to be announced.
